Frequently Asked Questions

A student can request leave by logging into the system with their credentials, navigating to the "Leave Request" section, and filling out the required information such as the reason for leave, start and end dates, and any supporting documents. Once the request is submitted, it will be sent to the designated authority for approval.

If your leave request is rejected, you will receive a notification stating the reason for rejection. You can then make any necessary adjustments and resubmit the request, or contact the authority for further clarification.
